Chess piece artwork bundled in this directory
=============================================

These traditional piece sets are sourced from the lichess.org open-source
project (lila), https://github.com/lichess-org/lila/tree/master/public/piece

  cburnett/  — by Colin M.L. Burnett. Licensed GPLv2+ (also available under
               BSD and GFDL). The standard Wikipedia Staunton set.
  merida/    — by Armando Hernandez Marroquin. Distributed under the GPL.
  maestro/   — from the lichess piece set collection, distributed under the GPL.

Each set is rendered as-is via <img> tags; no modifications were made to the
SVG files. Attribution retained per the respective licences.
